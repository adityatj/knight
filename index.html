Objective: The knight is placed at a random position on board when the game starts. Your goal is to cover all the tiles on the board by moving the knight (Standard chess 'L' move) and finally return to your original position from where you've started. (Indicated by a red tile). Also, the tiles disappear once the knight steps on them.
